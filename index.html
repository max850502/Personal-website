About

Bridging semiconductor engineering and materials research.

My background combines materials science, semiconductor testing, reliability engineering, and applied data analysis. I received my B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering from National Dong Hwa University and my M.S. in Engineering and System Science from National Tsing Hua University.

My long-term direction is to develop technologies that improve semiconductor test interfaces, power delivery stability, probe-card reliability, and advanced packaging performance through materials innovation and data-driven engineering.
