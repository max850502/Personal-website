Ambition Is a Quiet Force

Chapter 2 · Career

Ambition is often portrayed as something visible and intense — a constant drive to achieve more.

But in reality, the most enduring form of ambition is quiet. It is not expressed through bursts of effort, but through consistency over time.

It shows up in the decision to continue when progress is slow, and when results are not immediately visible.

This kind of ambition does not demand attention. It simply accumulates.
