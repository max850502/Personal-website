The Weight of Time with Family

Chapter 1 · Family

Time with family feels different as life becomes more complex.

What used to be natural and frequent slowly becomes limited and intentional. Schedules, responsibilities, and distance reshape how often people meet.

Because of that, each moment begins to carry more weight. Conversations become more meaningful, and presence becomes something you choose, not something that simply happens.

Perhaps that is the quiet transition into adulthood — when time is no longer abundant, but meaningful.
