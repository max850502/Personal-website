Why Family Still Matters

Chapter 1 · Family

In a world driven by performance, achievement, and progress, family can seem secondary.

There are always more goals to chase, more things to optimize, and more milestones to reach.

But in moments of uncertainty, it becomes clear that family is not about productivity. It is about stability.

It does not solve problems directly, but it provides a foundation — a place where things do not need to be earned or proven.
